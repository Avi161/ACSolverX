This folder  contains supplementary materials for the paper

A. Lisitsa, Stable Andrews-Curtis trivialization of AK(3) revisited.
A case study using automated deduction.  


Files 

AK3-stable.proof-1.txt 
AK3-stable.proof-2.txt 

contain two different proofs 
of AC-equivalence of the balanced trivial group presentation 

<a,b | a^{-1}b^{-1}ab^{-1}a^{-1}bab^{-2}aba^{-1}b, 
b^{-1}a^{-1}b^{2}a^{-1}b^{-1}abab^{-2}> 

to AK(3) trivial group presentation found by automated theorem proving. 

Files 

AK3-stable.proof-1-S1.txt
AK3-stable.proof-1-S2.txt
AK3-stable.proof-2-S5.txt

contain AC-transformation sequences S1, S2, S5, respectively, extracted from proofs.  (The sequences S3 and S4 are defined in terms of S2 in the paper above).
